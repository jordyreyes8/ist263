Injury Prevention

Foot and Ankle Injury Prevention

Foot and ankle injuries are usually caused by lack of foot support. Basketball shoes were made for this exact reason. However, not all basketball shoes offer the same type of support. The best way to prevent foot and ankle injuries are by selecting high top basketball shoes with a lot of ankle support. There are many low top and mid top basketball shoes, but they may not offer sufficient ankle support. Another way to assure your ankles are protected is by taping up your ankles with athletic tape.

Knee Injury Prevention

The best way to prevent knee injuries in basketball is by strengthening them in the gym. Some of the best different exercises you can do tare bodyweight squats, lateral lunges, crab walks, hip raises and ball leg curls.

Wrist and Hand Injury Prevention

The best way to prevent wrist and hand injuries is by being aware on the court. I’ve personally suffered from a wrist facture and I’m certain that if I was more aware of how I was landing I could’ve prevented the injury. It is important to be aware of other players on the court, the basketball, and how you land. If you fall while playing basketball it is crucial that you don’t land on your hands.

Head and Face Injury Prevention

There isn’t much you can do for head and face injuries but simply being careful. You should always be aware of your surroundings and should never get too aggressive on the court. A lot of these injuries happen on accident, so it is crucial that you be as careful as possible.
